Ginger Version 0.9.1
--------------------

More Analytical Functions
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
We have also implemented analytical functions for:

* power operator (**) 
* ceiling, floor, truncate
* abs
* asin, acos, atan, atan2
* cosh, sinh, tanh
* acosh, asinh, atanh
* gamma
* erf and erfc

Refactorings
~~~~~~~~~~~~

* All uses of Python2 have been replaced by Python3.
