The File Formats of the Ginger Toolchain Pipeline
-------------------------------------------------

* This section is intended to be the reference for the internal stages of the Ginger pipeline. 

* Readers of this section are likely to be trying to
  - Customise the Ginger IDE, hence they need to understand the formats in depth.

* It should certainly cover
  - MinX
  - GinX
  - Linx

* It specifically excludes the configuration files of particular tools, which should be documented in their own sections.
