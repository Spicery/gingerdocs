Documentation Guidelines for the Ginger Language
------------------------------------------------

* The Reader is likely to be reading this section to
  - Learn the Ginger language from scratch (tutorial needed)
  - Refresh their memory of some element of Ginger (index needed)
  - Compare the Ginger language with another language (cross-overs + overview)

* This is the definitive statement of the Ginger language.

* It should be sufficient for someone to read and write Ginger code.

* It should therefore include the standard libraries.
